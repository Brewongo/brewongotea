Our Story

Born in the tea gardens of India

Brewongo was born from a deep love for the rolling tea gardens of Assam, Darjeeling, and Nilgiris. We believe every cup of tea carries the soul of the land it was grown in — the morning mists, the careful hands of the picker, the slow patience of the brew. We ship these stories directly to you.

🌿 Ethically Sourced 🇮🇳 100% Indian Origin 📦 Direct to Door

The Heart Behind Brewongo

Founder & CEORiddhi Chowdhury

Riddhi grew up watching her grandmother brew the perfect cup of Assam tea every morning — loose leaf, unhurried, full of care. Years later, after studying business and building her career, she realized the world deserved to experience that same ritual. Not tea bags. Not dust. Real Indian tea, with provenance and pride. Brewongo is her love letter to the tea gardens of India — and to every person who believes the day begins better with a perfect brew.
